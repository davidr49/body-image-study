Get Involved

There are multiple ways to be involved with the Body Image and Ethnicity study this year.

If you don’t identify with any of the below opportunities, then please don’t hesitate to get in touch with Hannah.

Option 1

If you identify as female or non-binary, are aged 13-19, are from a South Asian background and experience body image concerns, you can attend a focus group to discuss your experiences.Just follow this link.

Option 2

If you meet the criteria for Option 1, but would rather have a 1:1 interview than attend a focus group, you canget in touch with Hannahto arrange a convenient time to chat.

Option 3

If you have lived experience of either BDD or an eating disorder, and would like to be involved in the co-production of a cultural adaptation, you canget in touch with Hannah here.
